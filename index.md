> **Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

soydantech ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application **Math Bunny** (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Device Information** | Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers |
| **Usage Information** | How you interact with the App, features used, time spent in the App |
| **Advertising Data** | Information related to advertisements displayed in the App |
| **App Preferences** | Language settings, game progress, level completion status |

### Information Stored Locally

Our App stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Game Progress**: Unlocked levels and sublevels for each math operation (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
- **Level Stars**: Stars earned for completing levels
- **Wrong Answers Data**: Questions you answered incorrectly, including wrong count and last wrong date (used to improve your learning experience)
- **Daily Analytics**: Daily statistics of correct and wrong answers (stored for 7 days only)
- **App Settings**: Language preference and other app preferences

This information is stored locally using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.
